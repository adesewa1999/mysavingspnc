Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password? +

To reset your password:

  1. Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page
  2. Enter your username and follow the verification steps
  3. Create a new strong password following our guidelines
  4. Confirm the new password and click "Submit"

You'll receive a confirmation email once your password has been successfully updated.

What should I do if I suspect fraudulent activity? +

If you notice any suspicious activity on your account:

  1. Immediately call our Fraud Prevention team at 1-877-287-2667
  2. Review your recent transactions and report any unauthorized activity
  3. Consider changing your online banking password
  4. Monitor your accounts closely for further unusual activity
How do I set up automatic bill payments? +

To set up automatic bill payments:

  1. Go to the "Pay Bills" section in online banking
  2. Select the biller you want to pay automatically
  3. Choose "Set Up Automatic Payments"
  4. Enter the payment amount and frequency
  5. Select the account to pay from
  6. Review and confirm your automatic payment details
What are the daily transfer limits? +

Our standard transfer limits are:

  • Between PNC accounts: $25,000 per day
  • To other banks (External Transfers): $5,000 per day
  • PNC PopmoneyÂ® transfers: $2,000 per day

Limits may vary based on your account type and history with PNC. You can view your specific limits in the "Transfer Money" section of online banking.

How do I update my contact information? +

To update your contact information:

  1. Sign in to online banking
  2. Go to "Profile & Settings"
  3. Select "Contact Information"
  4. Update your phone number, email, or mailing address
  5. Click "Save Changes"

For security reasons, some changes may require verification through a security code sent to your current contact information.
